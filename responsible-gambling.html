Responsible Gambling

Last updated: 29 June 2026
Gambling should be entertainment, never a way to make money or solve financial problems. If it stops being fun, it is time to stop. Predict2u is strictly for adults 18 and over.

Stay in control

  • Only ever bet money you can comfortably afford to lose.
  • Set a budget and a time limit before you start — and stick to them.
  • Never chase losses. Trying to win back money you have lost usually makes things worse.
  • Do not bet when stressed, upset, or under the influence of alcohol.
  • Treat any winnings as a bonus, not income you can count on.
  • Remember: no prediction, including ours, is ever a guarantee.

Warning signs

It may be time to seek help if you:

  • Bet more than you can afford, or borrow money to bet.
  • Feel anxious, guilty, or irritable about your gambling.
  • Hide your betting from family or friends.
  • Chase losses or bet to escape problems.
  • Find gambling is affecting your work, sleep, relationships, or finances.

Tools that help

  • Use deposit limits, time-outs, and self-exclusion features offered by licensed betting operators.
  • Take regular breaks. Step away from betting content, including ours.
  • Talk to someone you trust about how you are feeling.

Where to get help

If gambling is becoming a problem for you or someone you know, free and confidential support is available. You can reach out to a doctor, a mental-health professional, or a gambling-support organisation in your country. International resources include BeGambleAware and Gambling Therapy, which offers free online support worldwide.
