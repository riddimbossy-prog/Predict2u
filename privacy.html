Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This policy explains what limited information Predict2u handles. We keep things minimal by design.

1. We do not require accounts

Predict2u does not currently offer user accounts, logins, or registration. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or payment details to use the site.

2. Anonymous analytics

We use a privacy-first web analytics tool to understand general usage — for example, how many people visit and which pages are popular. This data is aggregated and anonymous. It does not identify you personally and does not use tracking cookies that follow you across other sites.

3. Information we do not collect

We do not sell data. We do not run advertising networks that profile you. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, and the site is strictly for adults 18 and over.

4. Third-party links

Our site links to external services such as Telegram and WhatsApp channels. Once you follow those links you are subject to their privacy policies, not ours. We have no control over how those platforms handle your data.

5. Hosting

The site is hosted on third-party infrastructure which may automatically log standard technical request data (such as IP address and browser type) for security and operational purposes, as is normal for any website. We do not use this to identify individuals.

6. Future changes

If we ever introduce accounts, email sign-ups, or payments, we will update this policy first to explain exactly what we collect, why, how it is protected, and how you can request deletion of your data. We will not start collecting personal data without telling you.

7. Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data today, there is nothing personal to access or delete. If this changes, you will have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold.
