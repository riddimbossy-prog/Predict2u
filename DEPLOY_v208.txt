Predict2U v208 deployment

Recommended: upload the contents of the FULL package to the repository root and replace matching files.

Changed-file deployment: upload every file from the CHANGED_FILES package to the repository root, preserving filenames.

After deployment:
1. Open Predict2U while online.
2. Refresh once or close and reopen the installed app.
3. The v208 service worker will clear the v207 caches.
4. Open News and confirm Source, Discuss, Read later, Share and Report are all visible.

Do not rename news-actions-v208.css; news.html loads it after the shared responsive stylesheet so its visibility rules win the cascade.
